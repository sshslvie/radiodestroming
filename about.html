Radio

Station de Stroming is a nomadic web radio station, conceived and presently based in Rotterdam. This station functions as a journalistic tool to design a series of conversations about and encounters, interrogating and revealing the current socio-political, legal context in which it is situated, as well as they ways in which this can be re-imagined.

The Dutch stroming translates to ‘the current’ and ‘ideological movement’.
A tension between water and time.

Season 1: Making - Rotterdam, the Netherlands, formed by and from water, moves in and out of focus. in the context of the graduation show fo the Lens-based Media Department of the Piet Zwart Institute in Rotterdam (2022). This radio station, initiated by slvie was set up as a research tool for the graduation show of the Lens-Based Department of the Piet Zwart Institute (2022).
