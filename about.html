Station de Stroming

is at once a nomadic web radio station and a journalistic platform, conceived in Rotterdam and presently based in France. This station functions as a tool to design a series of conversations about and encounters, interrogating and revealing the current socio-political, legal context in which it is situated, as well as they ways in which this can be re-imagined.

The Dutch stroming translates to ‘the current’ and ‘ideological movement’.
A tension between water and time.
