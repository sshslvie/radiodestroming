SEASON 1

This first season, rooted in Rotterdam, is produced within the context of the graduation show of the Lens-Based Media Department of the Piet Zwart Institute in Rotterdam (2022). This season's sonic journey starts in Rotterdam and like water, which holds and divides us, expands itself beyond national borders in the attempt to touch upon broader geo-political tides and imaginaries. Equally shifting through language. Rotterdam, formed by and from water, moves in and out of focus.
